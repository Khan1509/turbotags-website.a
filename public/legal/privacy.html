TurboTags ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website https://turbotags.app (the "Site"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site.

1. Information Collection

We collect information from you in the following ways:

  • Voluntary Information: We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us. The primary piece of information collected is the text prompt you enter into our generator to create tags and hashtags. This information is processed in real-time to provide the service and is not stored, logged, or associated with you in any way after the generation process is complete.
  • Analytics Data: We may use third-party service providers, such as Google Analytics, to collect standard internet log information and details of visitor behavior patterns. This is to understand aspects like the number of visitors to different parts of the site. This information is processed in a way that does not personally identify anyone.

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To operate and maintain our Site and services.
  • To generate tags and hashtags based on your input.
  • To improve our Site, services, and user experience.
  • To monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve your experience with the Site.

4. Third-Party Services

Our tag generation is powered by the OpenRouter API. We send your prompt to their service for processing. We encourage you to review their privacy policy and contact them directly for responses to your questions. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

5. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information from our files.
